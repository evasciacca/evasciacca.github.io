Bio

I am a computer scientist and tecnologist post-doc INAF Astrophysical Observatory of Catania. My research focuses on Web Technologies and Scientific Visualization for astrophysical applications. Previous research topics include: Systems Biology, Computational biological modeling and design, Linguistic models in Bioinformatics, Mathematical models for semiconductor devices, Electronic Circuit Design. Previously, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the University of Turin, advised by Professor Mario Coppo, where I developed computational methods for designing and analysing models of plant biology. I obtained a Ph.D. in Mathematics for Technologu from the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science of the University of Catania in 2010, advised by Professor Giuseppe Nicosia, working on optimization methods for biological and electronic systems design.
